Beat the Goblin!

The website

Beat the Goblin is a gamified website designed to help you manage your daily tasks with ease.

Our goal is to gently nudge you into completing your tasks without the stress of feeling punished if you can't finish everything on time. With a soft and wholesome design, we create a playful and peaceful atmosphere that makes task management feel less like a chore and more like an adventure.

And if you happen to miss a deadline, don’t worry!
We’ve got solutions to get you back on track, ensuring that every step of your journey feels supportive and encouraging.

To be continued...

The future of Beat the Goblin is bright and full of exciting possibilities!

While the website is still under construction, I'm working hard to roll out some extra features that will enhance your experience. Soon, you can look forward to streaks and rewards that will keep you motivated as you complete your tasks. I'm also planning to introduce weekly task management tools, making it easier to organize your weeks. And for those who love a little friendly competition, leaderboards will be added to track your progress and celebrate achievements.

Stay tuned—there's so much more to come!
